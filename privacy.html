Privacy Policy for Clipboard Manager

Effective Date: November 1, 2025

Clipboard Manager is a privacy-focused Chrome extension that operates entirely within your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All clipboard content, tags, and settings are saved locally on your device using Chrome’s storage.local API.

What We Do

  • Store copied text, links, or code snippets only on your device.
  • Allow you to search, edit, and organize your clipboard items.
  • Optionally display metadata (favicon, title) for copied URLs.

What We Don’t Do

  • We do not track users or use analytics.
  • We do not send or share your data with any third parties.
  • We do not make network requests or use remote databases.
  • We do not use cookies, ads, or marketing identifiers.

Data Deletion

All information is stored locally on your device. Clearing your browser data or uninstalling the extension will permanently delete all stored clips, tags, and settings.
